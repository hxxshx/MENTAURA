Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

To create an account, click on the "Signup" button in the top-right corner of any page. Fill out the registration form with your full name, email address, and create a secure password.

MentAura offers professional counseling sessions, 24/7 AI-powered support, community forums, mental health resources, and educational content.

MentAura offers both free and premium services. Basic features like AI support and community forums are free. Professional counseling sessions require payment.

Counseling & Sessions

After logging in, click "Book Session" from the navigation menu. Choose from available therapists, select your preferred date and time, and complete the booking.

Yes, all our counselors are licensed mental health professionals with proper credentials and extensive experience in their respective fields.

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel appointments up to 24 hours before your scheduled session through your account dashboard.

AI Support

Yes, our AI support is available 24/7 to provide immediate assistance and guidance whenever you need it.

Our AI is trained on extensive mental health data and provides evidence-based responses. However, it's designed to supplement, not replace, professional therapy.

Privacy & Security

We use industry-standard security protocols including SSL encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits to protect your personal information.

Yes, all interactions with our AI and human therapists are completely confidential and protected under strict privacy guidelines.

Technical Support

Yes, MentAura is fully mobile-responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

MentAura works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated.

Contact our technical support team immediately through the contact form or email support@mentaura.com for quick assistance.
