Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect your data.

Introduction

At MentAura, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health platform and services.

By using MentAura, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described here, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name, email address, and contact details
  • Account credentials and login information
  • Profile information and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely by third parties)

Health-Related Information

  • Mental health assessments and survey responses
  • Session notes and therapeutic communications
  • Progress tracking and wellness goals
  • Crisis intervention data (when applicable)

Usage Data

  • App usage patterns and interactions
  • Device information and IP addresses
  • Session duration and feature utilization
  • Communication with support team

How We Use Your Information

Provide Services

Deliver mental health services, AI support, and counseling sessions

Safety & Security

Ensure user safety, prevent fraud, and maintain platform security

Improve Platform

Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services and user experience

Communication

Send important updates, notifications, and support messages

Information Sharing

We Do Not Sell Your Data

MentAura never sells, rents, or trades your personal or health information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect the safety of users in emergency situations.

Anonymized Data

We may use aggregated, anonymized data for research purposes to improve mental health services industry-wide.

Data Security

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols

Secure Servers

Data stored on secure, regularly updated servers with access controls

Access Control

Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis for authorized personnel

Regular Audits

Regular security audits and monitoring to identify and address vulnerabilities

Your Privacy Rights

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances

Right to Object

Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes

Data Portability

Request transfer of your data to another service provider

Right to Restrict

Request restriction of processing under certain conditions

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze usage, and provide personalized content. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

Essential Cookies

Required for basic platform functionality and security

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users interact with our platform

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and personalize your experience

Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.

Account Information

Retained while your account is active, plus 3 years after closure

Health Data

Retained according to healthcare regulations and your consent

Usage Analytics

Typically retained for 2 years in anonymized form
